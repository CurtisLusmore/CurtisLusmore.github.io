  • The metaphor I like to use is the film and TV industry
  • Hire the best, don’t rely on formulae
  • Pilot, renew in installments, invest in what works, can what doesn’t, profits fund future work
  • Engage with audience, find out what they like, make it
  • Get a feedback loop and always be willing to change or adapt
  • Nobody would write all nine seasons, find whatever cast you can afford, film and produce it all, and then release it
  • As an extreme example, sports gambling, or tipping competitions in particular
  • Of course you think about it at the start of the season
  • But you would never consider locking in your tips before the season starts
  • Incorporate new information as it becomes available
  • Be prepared to change your plans
