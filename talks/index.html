Accidental Agility

Curtis Lusmore
28 February 2019

This month marked the 18th anniversary of the famous meeting at Snowbird which resulted in the Agile Software Development Manifesto! Agile has exploded since then to take the business world by storm, but even some of the original authors are concerned we’ve lost sight of the original goals.

In this talk, I try to rediscover the essence of agility by taking inspiration from another industry that independently discovered similar techniques.
