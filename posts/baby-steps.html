Baby Steps

One of the things that you do when you become a parent for the first time is you imagine what your child’s first word will be. I remember thinking that the most likely words would be mama or dada, but with so many words to choose from, the likelihood would be that it would be some other random word like book, shoe or head.

Curtis Lusmore, 31 January 2023
